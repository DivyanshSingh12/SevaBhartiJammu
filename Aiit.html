Our Initiatives/ Education

Sewa Bharti is one of the largest Non-Governmental organizations (NGOs) working for the upliftment and empowerment of the neglected and underprivileged sections of society, particularly Empowerment of communities, Secure sustainable livelihoods, Promotion of micro-finance, Promotion of societal peace, Alternative energy option, Community based calamity preparedness, Promotion of people’s culture etc.

Introduction

It is foreseen that each and every section of the society shall use Information Technology as a service in future. The digital divide between computer literate and illiterates is increasing day by day. The Union Government of the day is increasing using governance in its day-to-day operation whereby delivery of such citizen centric services can be given to the end users effectively. Thus the Government shall become sensitive, responsive and citizen friendly

Objectives

Sewa Bharti has now taken up task to remove the digital divide between computer literate and illiterates by opening, supporting and making available computer centres in Sewa Bastis and giving computer education, training and knowledge of Information Technology. The main objectives behind computer center project is to provide computer training to boys and girls of Sewa Bastis, rehabilitated bastis and backward regions to make them self-reliant and to impart “SANSKARS” in them.

    Provide Computer training to children from Sewa Bastis to make them self reliant

    Impart “SANSKARS” (values) in them

    Remove the digital gap between computer literate and illiterates

Operations

At present, there are 02 computer centres in Jammu Region where computer training is provided to boys and girls of Sewa Basti’s, rehabilitated bastis and backward regions to make them self-reliant and to impart “SANSKARS” in them. Placement facility is also provided to them. Discussions are organized on social, religious, patriotic and different issues on regular basis to impart “SANSKAR” to them.
