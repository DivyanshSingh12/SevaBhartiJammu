Sewa Bharti Assumes Charge of Viraj Bal Bhawan
School in Khanpur, Nagrota, Jammu & Kashmir

In a significant move towards empowering the youth and strengthening educational foundations in Jammu & Kashmir, Sewa Bharti, a renowned NGO dedicated to social upliftment, has officially taken over the management of Viraj Bal Bhawan School, Khanpur, Nagrota. This momentous occasion marks the beginning of a new era for the school, which has been a pillar of education in the region.

The Handing Over and Taking Over (HOTO) ceremony was conducted with great fervour, signifying a seamless transition of leadership. The formal transfer of responsibilities took place between Shri Rakesh Jha, President of Sewa Bharti, and Smt. Raj Bharti (Sharma), in the esteemed presence of senior Sewa Bharti officials including Shri T.S. Negi, Vice President, Shri Pradeep Kapoor, General Secretary, and Shri Ajay Takoo, Treasurer, among other distinguished guests.

In his address, Shri Rakesh Jha President Sewa Bharti J&K expressed his deep commitment to the mission of Sewa Bharti and outlined a visionary roadmap for the future of Viraj Bal Bhawan School. "Our goal is to transform this institution into a beacon of excellence, where every child’s potential is nurtured, and holistic development is prioritized. We are committed to upholding the values of quality education and inclusivity, ensuring that every student here receives the best possible opportunities to excel," he remarked.

Smt. Raj Bharti (Sharma) , who played a pivotal role in the school’s journey until now, shared her confidence in Sewa Bharti’s ability to lead the school into a brighter future. She thanked the school’s staff, students, and parents for their unwavering support and dedication, expressing her optimism for the continued growth and success of the institution under its new management.

The event was further enlivened by a series of captivating cultural performances by the students of Viraj Bal Bhawan School. These performances, which included traditional dances, songs, and skits, were a testament to the rich cultural heritage of the region and the immense talent of the students. The cultural showcase not only entertained the audience but also underscored the importance of preserving cultural traditions in educational settings.

Shri T.S. Negi, Vice President of Sewa Bharti, also addressed the gathering, emphasizing the NGO’s longstanding commitment to education and community development. He encouraged the students to strive for excellence in all spheres of life and assured them of Sewa Bharti’s unwavering support in their academic and personal growth.

Shri Pradeep Kapoor, General Secretary, and Shri Ajay Takoo, Treasurer, echoed similar sentiments, highlighting the importance of a collaborative effort between the school’s management, teachers, and students to achieve the shared vision of educational excellence.
